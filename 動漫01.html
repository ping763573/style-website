關於我

About Me

我是小櫻，專業動漫插畫師

I'm Sakura, Professional Anime Illustrator

從小熱愛動漫與繪畫，畢業於東京設計學院動漫設計系。擁有5年以上專業插畫經驗，擅長角色設計、場景繪製與故事板創作。

Passionate about anime and drawing since childhood, graduated from Tokyo Design Institute with a degree in Anime Design. With over 5 years of professional illustration experience, specializing in character design, scene drawing, and storyboard creation.

我的風格融合了傳統日式動漫與現代插畫技巧，特別擅長表現角色情感與故事性場景。曾參與多部動畫製作與遊戲角色設計。

My style combines traditional Japanese anime with modern illustration techniques, especially good at expressing character emotions and storytelling scenes. Participated in several animation productions and game character designs.
