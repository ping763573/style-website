關於攝影師 About the Photographer

陳光影Chen Guangying

電影寫真攝影師 | 15年經驗Cinematic Photographer | 15 Years Experience

我是一名熱愛光影的電影寫真攝影師，沉迷於捕捉那些難以言喻的瞬間。我的攝影風格深受經典電影的影響，追求畫面中的敘事性與情感張力。

過去15年，我曾與眾多電影製作團隊、廣告公司及藝術家合作，累積了豐富的拍攝經驗。無論是人像、風景還是商業攝影，我都堅持以電影鏡頭的思維來構圖，讓每一張照片都像一幀電影畫面般充滿故事感。 I am a cinematic photographer passionate about light and shadow, obsessed with capturing those ineffable moments. My photography style is deeply influenced by classic films, pursuing narrative and emotional tension in every frame.

Over the past 15 years, I've collaborated with numerous film crews, advertising agencies, and artists, accumulating rich shooting experience. Whether it's portrait, landscape, or commercial photography, I坚持 to compose with a cinematic mindset, making every photo as storytelling as a movie frame.
