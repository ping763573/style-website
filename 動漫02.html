關於我 About Me

我的故事與技能 My Story & Skills

我是一名熱愛動漫的藝術家，從小就對日本動漫充滿熱情。多年來，我專注於角色設計、場景繪製和短篇動畫製作，努力創造出充滿生命力的動漫世界。 I'm an anime enthusiast and artist who has been passionate about Japanese animation since childhood. Over the years, I've focused on character design, scene illustration, and short animation production, striving to create vibrant anime worlds.

我相信每個角色都有自己的故事，每個場景都有獨特的氛圍。透過我的作品，我希望能帶給觀眾情感上的共鳴和視覺上的享受。 I believe every character has their own story and every scene has a unique atmosphere. Through my work, I hope to bring emotional resonance and visual enjoyment to the audience.
