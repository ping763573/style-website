關於我 About Me

我是一位熱愛像素藝術的創作者，有超過5年的像素繪圖經驗。曾參與多個獨立遊戲項目的美術設計，擅長復古8-bit和16-bit風格的角色與場景設計。 I'm a pixel art enthusiast with over 5 years of experience in pixel drawing. I've participated in the art design of several indie game projects, specializing in retro 8-bit and 16-bit style character and scene design.

除了像素藝術，我也精通網頁設計與開發，能夠將像素美學完美融入現代網站中，創造出獨特的視覺體驗。 In addition to pixel art, I'm also proficient in web design and development, able to perfectly integrate pixel aesthetics into modern websites to create a unique visual experience.
