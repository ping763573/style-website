關於我 About Me

水彩藝術家 Watercolor Artist

我是一位充滿熱情的水彩藝術家，擁有超過十年的繪畫經驗。我熱愛透過水彩的流動性和透明感來捕捉自然與生活中的美好瞬間。 我的作品融合了傳統技法與現代審美，希望能帶給觀賞者平靜與感動。 I am a passionate watercolor artist with over ten years of painting experience. I love capturing beautiful moments in nature and life through the fluidity and transparency of watercolors. My works blend traditional techniques with modern aesthetics, hoping to bring peace and inspiration to viewers.

除了創作，我也從事水彩教學工作，幫助更多人發現水彩畫的魅力。無論是風景、人物還是抽象題材，我都樂於探索與挑戰。 In addition to creating art, I also teach watercolor painting, helping more people discover the charm of this medium. Whether it's landscapes, portraits, or abstract themes, I enjoy exploring and challenging myself.

我的服務 My Services

水彩創作 Watercolor Creation

接受客製化水彩繪畫委託，包括肖像、風景、寵物等題材，為您創作獨一無二的藝術作品。 Accepting custom watercolor painting commissions, including portraits, landscapes, pets, and other subjects, creating unique works of art for you.
